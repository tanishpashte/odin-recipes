Caprese Salad

Description

Fresh and vibrant, the Caprese Salad is a classic Italian dish that celebrates the flavors of ripe tomatoes, fresh mozzarella, and basil. This simple salad is drizzled with balsamic glaze for an extra burst of flavor, making it a perfect appetizer or side dish.

Ingredients

  • 4 large ripe tomatoes, sliced
  • 1 pound fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • 1 bunch fresh basil leaves
  • Â¼ cup extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons balsamic glaze
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Arrange alternating slices of tomatoes and mozzarella on a serving platter.
  2. Tuck fresh basil leaves between the tomato and mozzarella slices.
  3. Drizzle extra-virgin olive oil and balsamic glaze over the salad. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Serve immediately as a refreshing appetizer or a side dish to complement your meal.
